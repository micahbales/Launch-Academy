### Introduction

Build a Ruby program that simulates rolling a pair (2) of dice.


### Sample Run

```no-highlight
$ ruby dice.rb
You rolled a 1 and a 5
Total: 6
Press 'Enter' to roll again, 'S' to stop

You rolled a 5 and a 6
Total: 11
Press 'Enter' to roll again, 'S' to stop

You rolled a 2 and a 6
Total: 8
Press 'Enter' to roll again, 'S' to stop

You rolled a 2 and a 1
Total: 3
Press 'Enter' to roll again, 'S' to stop
S
```
